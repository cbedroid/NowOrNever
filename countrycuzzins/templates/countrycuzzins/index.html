Country Rap Tunes

Country Cuzzins has their own, original flavor, from Mississippi. A sound that consist of rap, blues, soul and melodic tunes, that they call "Country Rap Tunes" in respect to a Down South Legend "Pimp C". Coming from the poorest state in America, Their lyrics are based on the everyday struggles of living in the ghetto.

About County Cuzzins

From the dirty south, born and raised in Greenwood/Jackson, Ms. They came up performing for family members and so on. They was introduced to blues, soul, rap and other music as they were coming up that influenced their style of music. They also looked up too Southern rap groups such as Outkast, UGK, 8ball and MJG and recently Young thug and rich homie Quan.

Be Inspired by Everything

Country Cuzzins inspiration came from the death of BottomBoyCort's brother Robert "Spud" Brooks. They begin sharing their pain through their music. Becoming a house hold name, they just want to be seen as Different from the rest and in their own lane. The whole southern rap culture is what inspired them to put on.
